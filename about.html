About Us

Our Vision

It’s a web-based application which helps the needy user to buy or sell used books. Users can take pictures of their books and upload them to the website. Buyer can search either by author name or book title or by subject. It covers the schools, universities, and people who are avid readers.

Our Mission

It is a very effective idea that is capable of saving time and cost for any individual who can be an avid reader or a teacher and student according to their need. To find their book customer do not blindly go to various shops or markets. It helps the seller to save at least 50% cost of the book price and allows the buyer to maintain their book habit.
